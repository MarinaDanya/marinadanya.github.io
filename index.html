Short Bio

I am a second year PhD student at Institute of Control Sciences of Russian Academy of Sciences working under the supervision of Boris Polyak. Prior to this, I earned MSc from Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology and Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology and BSc from Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology. Also, from 2018 I am a junior researcher at  Laboratory of Numerical Methods of Applied Structural Optimization, MIPT and from 2019 I am doing research at Huawei-MIPT group. My research interests include convex optimization, first-order methods, huge-scale optimization and stochastic optimization.
